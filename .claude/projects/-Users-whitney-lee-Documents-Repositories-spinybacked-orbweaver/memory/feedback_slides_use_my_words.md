Use Whitney's exact words when building slide content. Light refinements for voice-to-text artifacts are okay, but never rewrite the spirit of what she said. Don't add words she didn't say unless she asks for refinement.

**Why:** Whitney writes her own talk content and uses voice-to-text. The slides should sound like her, not like an AI rewrote them.

**How to apply:** When converting notes to slides, pull phrases directly from the notes. Clean up grammar/typos from dictation, but preserve her voice, phrasing, and word choices.
